Bringing long distance friendships closer

Our Goal:

    To help people stay connected with their long distance friendships
    by helping them create meaningful memories and making it easier to talk with each other.

Our solution:

    We designed a phone app that emphasizes one-on-one relationships with people.
    Our major focuses were to:

  • Have face-to-face communication through video calls
  • Easily schedule video calls while friends are in different time zones
  • Create and share one-on-one memories with your closest friends
