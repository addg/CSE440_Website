bringing long-distance friendships closer

our goal:

    We aim to help people stay connected with their long-distance friendships
    by helping them create meaningful memories and making it easy to talk with each other.

our solution:

    After thorough research and testing, we have designed a one of a kind app
    that emphasizes deep one-on-one friendships. We focused on:

  • Video Calling for face-to-face communication
  • Scheduling Video Calls around time zones
  • Creating and reflecting upon one-on-one memories with your closest friends
design


best friends

When you enter the app, the first thing you see are your best friends and a map of where they are around the world. Even though you aren't close to them physically, you can check up on them whenever. All the main functions are within the + sign and pop out as “add friend”, “create memory” and “schedule video call”.

friendship

This is where you can see everything about your friendship with another friend. Here you can schedule a video call with them, look through some old memories, or check out your shared timeline of memories and milestones.

scheduling

Because we know you have friends all over, we accommodate for that in our scheduler. Taking your time zone and theirs, we are able to let you know the exact time difference between you and your friend when scheduling a Voice Call. Before a Video Call is set, it must be confirmed by the other friend. You can send an edit request for a changed time, and you may also delete the Video Call if need be.

memory

Every moment with your best friend is special and we want both of you to treasure that with our memories feature. You can create a memory with a friend, fill it with photos, datemark it, and cherish it forever. If you need to, it’s easy to move around, add and delete the photos in the Memory.
